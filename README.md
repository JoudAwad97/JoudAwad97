## 👨‍💻 About Me

With over **11 years** in the software industry, I design and deliver high-performance, production-grade systems at scale. I currently architect systems serving **3 million daily active users**, leading **20+ microservices** across 5 distributed teams on AWS.

I specialize in **Agentic AI and multi-agent orchestration systems** — building production-grade RAG pipelines, integrating LLMs, and deploying ML models for fraud detection, recommendation engines, and personalized search. My deep expertise spans **AWS (6x certified including SA Professional & DevOps Professional)**, **GCP**, **Kubernetes (CKA + CKAD)**, and **Terraform (certified)**.

On the backend, I architect end-to-end **cloud-native solutions** across multi-cloud environments, with deep hands-on experience in **API design** (REST, GraphQL, gRPC), **microservices architecture**, **event-driven systems** (Kafka, RabbitMQ, EventBridge), and **container orchestration** with Docker and Kubernetes. On the data layer, I bring extensive experience across relational and NoSQL databases — including PostgreSQL, DynamoDB, MongoDB, Cassandra, Neptune, and Redis — ensuring optimal data modeling, performance, and scalability for complex distributed systems.

**Key results:** Reduced cloud operational costs by 30%. Improved system performance by 40%. Led teams of up to 12 engineers. Directed end-to-end cloud migrations from legacy monoliths to microservices architecture.

I write about architecture and AI on my **[technical blog](https://medium.com/@joudwawad)** (3,600+ followers), covering MCP servers, Agentic AI systems, and system design at scale.

**Open to Principal Solution Architecture or Principal Backend Engineering remote roles globally.**
